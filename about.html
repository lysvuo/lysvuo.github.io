ABOUT ME



Hi! I'm LySandra Vuong. I'm a Los Angeles based artist, designer, and student. I'm a lover of creating and design thinking, and am pursuing these passions by majoring in biomedical engineering and minoring in communication design at the University of Southern California.

When I'm not studying, I'm working on my art and expanding my portfolio. I am currently accepting commissions in web design, graphic design, product branding, and artwork. I am very open-minded and willing to try new things and acquire new skills, so please feel free to inquire about other art services. I would lov to collaborate with you!
